Information about New Zealand

New Zealand, known for its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture, is a must-visit destination. From the majestic fjords of Milford Sound to the geothermal wonders of Rotorua, there's something for every traveler. The country offers a mix of outdoor adventures, rich Maori heritage, and modern cities like Auckland and Wellington.

Popular attractions

  • Milford Sound: Experience spectacular canals and waterfalls.
  • Rotorua: See geothermal wonders and Maori culture.
  • Mount Cook: Enjoy the view over New Zealands tallest mountains.

Exciting activities

  • Hiking in the National Park
  • Visit lakes
  • Taste local favorites

Tips

Get the most out of your visit by following our insider tips:

  • Best time to visit: November-April
  • Hidden gems: Wanaka, Abel Tasman nationalpark
  • Transport: Rent a car to explore freely, or visit G Adventures to book a trip
